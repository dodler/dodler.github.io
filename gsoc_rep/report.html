OrientDB connector for Teiiid.

OrientDB is nosql database that supports graphs, documents and some other features. It has also a jdbc driver, what gives an opportunity to reuse existing jdbc connection factory. However define connection between teiid and orientdb is quite challenging and not obvious. Thanks to my mentor (Ramesh Reddy), who helped me a lot with it.

What is done:

added factory for connection and some modifiers

blog entry - teiid start guide

blog entry - performance comparison
